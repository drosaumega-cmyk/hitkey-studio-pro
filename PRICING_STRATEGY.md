# AI Audio Studio Pro - Comprehensive Pricing Strategy

## Executive Summary

AI Audio Studio Pro implements a token-based subscription model designed for global scalability while maintaining competitive pricing in the US market. The strategy balances user acquisition through free/trial offerings with premium monetization tiers and flexible token purchasing options.

## Core Pricing Philosophy

### Token-Based Consumption Model
- **Microtransactions**: Users pay per actual usage rather than flat fees
- **Fair Pricing**: Heavy users pay more, casual users pay less
- **Predictable Costs**: Users can control spending through token management
- **Feature Flexibility**: Different features consume different token amounts

### Global Market Adaptation
- **Regional Pricing**: Adjusted for local purchasing power
- **Currency Support**: 25+ currencies with real-time conversion
- **Cultural Sensitivity**: Pricing adapted to local market expectations
- **Competitive Positioning**: Priced competitively in each region
